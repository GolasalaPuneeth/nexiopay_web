Client Success Stories

Real-world IoT and smart automation solutions transforming education.

Magik Mat

The Magik Mat is an interactive educational play mat designed to make learning both engaging and enjoyable for children aged 3 to 8. It allows kids to participate in educational games while physically jumping and interacting with the mat.

Powered by Bluetooth connectivity, the system integrates seamlessly with Android TVs and laptops, transforming traditional learning into a dynamic, movement-based experience. Nexiopay supported the technology architecture and automation framework behind this smart learning solution.

My Talking Tree

My Talking Tree is an AI-integrated robotic tree designed to enhance learning experiences in schools and public spaces. Through speech interaction, expressive movements, and engaging educational content, it creates an immersive learning environment.

The system promotes tech-integrated engagement, joyful learning, skillful development, and creativity empowerment. Nexiopay contributed to the automation logic, AI integration framework, and smart interaction systems powering this innovative solution.
